Welcome, or as we say in Ireland fáilte, to my portfolio website.


From a young age I was fortunate to encounter various forms of technology and try my hand at multiple coding languages. But over the last 5 years I was able to work for an IT company who are both the service provider and operator for online gambling website, among other ventures.


While the role was a Client Support position, assisting users with account issues, queries and troubleshooting on said website, I was able to connect with members of the various IT teams within the company. It was thanks to this I was able to pick up skills that, while not directly involved in coding, networking, or the likes, it allowed for me to better understand how the systems operated and develop a more troubleshooting, and analytical-focused mindset that let me look at issues and interactions from a different perspective and come up with an efficient resolution where possible.


Before the madness that was the year 2020, I was applying for a Data Analysis course, as I was developing an interest in that area and felt that the experience would have been a great. Unfortunately, that course never went ahead but I was offered a place in their Software Development course in its stead. It was thanks to this course that I believed I could progress further with a career in this industry, and it is also the reason I discovered HZ University of Applied Science’s ICT course. As one of my assignments we were tasked with discovering progressions opportunities and I made the decision to look outside of my country’s education system and look at overseas opportunities.


HZ’s ICT course offered opportunities for me to study both Software Engineering and Data Science, as well as develop Business IT skills through the course’s content and assignments. Add this to their focus on hands on, practical learning, compared to a more theoretical approach, I believed that this type of course suited my learning styles as well as provide plenty of opportunities to learn new skills and develop upon my existing skillset. With my mind set on taking this course, I took the leap by leaving my job, packing my bags and moving from my home, in the valley of Cork City, Ireland to the beautiful flatlands of Middelburg, The Netherlands.


This website will act as a portfolio of the work I will complete during my studies in the coming years showing the following

  • Study Progress
  • Records of my learning and personal development (Blog)
  • Course and College related information
